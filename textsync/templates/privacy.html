Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 31, 2025

AutoText ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome browser extension.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Username
  • Password (encrypted and securely stored)
  • Authentication tokens

1.2 User-Generated Content

We collect and store the text shortcuts and expansions that you create, including:

  • Shortcut keys (trigger words)
  • Expansion text (plain text and HTML formatted content)
  • Organization of shortcuts into sets
  • Metadata (creation date, last modified date)

1.3 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about your use of the extension:

  • Synchronization timestamps
  • Selected shortcut sets
  • Extension configuration preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide core functionality: Enable text expansion and synchronization across your devices
  • User authentication: Verify your identity and maintain your session
  • Data synchronization: Keep your shortcuts up-to-date across all your devices
  • Service improvement: Monitor and improve the extension's performance and reliability
  • Technical support: Respond to your support requests and technical issues

3. Data Storage and Security

We take data security seriously:

  • Local Storage: Shortcuts are cached locally in your browser's storage for offline access
  • Server Storage: Your data is stored on secure servers with encryption at rest
  • Transmission Security: All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Password Protection: Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms
  • Access Control: Only you can access your shortcuts unless you explicitly share them

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent: When you explicitly share shortcut sets with other users
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Service protection: To protect the rights, property, or safety of AutoText, our users, or the public

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export: Download your shortcuts in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Stop using the extension at any time by uninstalling it

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. When you delete your account:

  • All your shortcuts and personal data are permanently deleted from our servers
  • Locally cached data is removed when you uninstall the extension
  • Backups are purged within 30 days

7. Children's Privacy

AutoText is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

9. Third-Party Services

AutoText does not integrate with or share data with third-party analytics or advertising services. The extension operates independently without external tracking.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending a notification through the extension (for significant changes)

11. Browser Permissions

AutoText requires the following Chrome permissions:

  • Storage: To save your shortcuts locally for offline access
  • All URLs: To enable text expansion on all websites you visit
  • Alarms: For periodic synchronization of shortcuts
  • Notifications: To inform you about sync status and important updates

These permissions are used solely for the extension's core functionality and not for data collection or tracking.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Website: https://autotext.zua.ro
  • Email: privacy@zua.ro

We will respond to all inquiries within 30 days.

13. Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies

By using AutoText, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
